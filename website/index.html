Why Shopify Brands Lose Revenue

The hidden signal degradation costing you 11-20% of your data

iOS 14+ Privacy Changes

Apple's ATT framework blocks up to 85% of user tracking on iOS devices. Without proper server-side implementation, your Meta Pixel is blind to most conversions.

Impact: 15-40% of purchases invisible

Ad Blockers & Browser Restrictions

30%+ of users run ad blockers that prevent GA4 and GTM from loading. Safari's ITP limits cookie lifespan to 7 days, breaking attribution windows.

Impact: 8-15% data loss on browsers

Consent Banner Misconfig

Improperly configured cookie consent banners block tracking before users even interact. Default "reject all" implementations destroy data collection.

Impact: 20-60% of EU traffic untracked

Shopify Checkout Limitations

Standard Shopify checkout restricts custom JavaScript. Without Plus or proper workarounds, purchase events often fire incorrectly or not at all.

Impact: 5-25% purchase events missing

Cross-Domain Tracking Gaps

Subdomains, third-party checkouts, and payment redirects break session continuity. Users appear as new visitors, inflating acquisition metrics.

Impact: 10-30% misattributed sessions

GTM/GA4 Config Errors

Duplicate tags, incorrect triggers, and broken data layers are endemic. Most implementations have 3-7 silent errors firing on every page.

Impact: Data accuracy reduced 15-40%
